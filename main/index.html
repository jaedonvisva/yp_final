Welcome to Youth Professionals!

Who are we?

Youth Professionals is an international nonprofit organization dedicated towards supporting BIPOC and LGBTQ+ youth with their identities and goals. After noticing the vast disparities between BIPOC and LGBTQ+ youth, Youth Professionals decided to address these issues. Founded in the summer of 2020, Youth Professionals provides youth with essential leadership skills. Through webinars, workshops and engaging social media posts, Youth Professionals hopes to welcome youth all over the world.
